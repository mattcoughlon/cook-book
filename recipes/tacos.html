Simple tacos

Here's a simple way to make tacos.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Peppers
  • Onion
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Brown and season the beef.
  2. Crisp the tortillas.
  3. Saute the pepper and onion.
  4. Grate the cheese.
  5. Add the beef to the shells and add your preffered toppings.
