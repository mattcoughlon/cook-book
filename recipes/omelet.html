Easy Cheese Omelet

Here's how I make my omelets:

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Cheese
  • mushrooms

Steps

  1. beat the eggs
  2. heat pan on medium
  3. grate cheese
  4. saute mushrooms
  5. scrammble eggs, while maintaining a pancake shape
  6. add cheese and mushrooms to 1 half of the pancake
  7. close the omelet
  8. flip
